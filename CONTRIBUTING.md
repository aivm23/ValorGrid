# Contributing to ValorGrid Community

ValorGrid Community is licensed under the Mozilla Public License 2.0 (`MPL-2.0`).

By contributing a change, you confirm that:

- you have the right to submit the contribution;
- your contribution is submitted under `MPL-2.0`;
- the contribution does not include secrets, credentials, private portfolio data, broker exports or material copied from incompatible sources.

Small fixes and documentation improvements can be submitted under the normal repository license. Larger contributions may require an additional contributor agreement before they are accepted, especially if they affect licensing, commercial packaging, Professional Edition integration points or long-term relicensing flexibility.

Do not submit Professional Edition code, broker-specific parser internals, private fixtures, local paths, credentials or user portfolio data to this public Community repository.
